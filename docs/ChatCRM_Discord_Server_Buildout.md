# ChatCRM Discord Server Buildout

## Goal
Create a clean team hub for the ChatCRM acquisition team. Keep it simple: announcements, training, daily calling, support, wins, and leadership.

## Server Name
ChatCRM Team

## Server Tagline
Close More. Build Wealth.

## Roles
Create these roles in this order:

1. Founder / Admin
   - Virgo Davis
   - Shawn McGraw
   - Full access

2. Acquisition Caller
   - Calling team
   - Can access training, call floor, scripts, wins, questions, and announcements

3. Disposition
   - Buyer side team
   - Can access buyers, dispo, deal updates, and general team areas

4. VA / Support
   - Can access support, training, data cleanup, and assigned work areas

5. Trainee
   - New team members before paperwork is signed
   - Read-only access to welcome, rules, onboarding, and training-call-info

## Channel Purpose

### welcome
Use this for first impressions and orientation.

Message:
Welcome to ChatCRM. This is where we train, communicate, track wins, and build the acquisition team.

Start here:
1. Read rules-and-expectations.
2. Check training-call-info.
3. Complete your ChatCRM login.
4. Sign your paperwork inside ChatCRM.
5. Review call-script and rebuttals.
6. Introduce yourself in daily-check-in.

Close More. Build Wealth.

### rules-and-expectations
Message:
ChatCRM Team Expectations

1. Protect all seller, buyer, and company information.
2. Do not share leads, scripts, data, or internal training outside the team.
3. Keep notes clean and accurate inside ChatCRM.
4. Do not quote prices or make promises unless leadership approves it.
5. Be respectful, professional, and coachable.
6. Show up on time for training and scheduled calling blocks.
7. Ask questions early. Do not guess on important seller information.
8. Every call matters.

### sign-paperwork
Message:
Before calling live leads, every team member must complete onboarding in ChatCRM.

Steps:
1. Log in with the account provided.
2. Enter your name and email.
3. Review the agreement.
4. Sign electronically.
5. Download your signed copy.

Admin will be able to see when your paperwork is complete.

## Simple Onboarding Flow

1. New caller joins Discord.
2. Give them Trainee role.
3. They attend training.
4. They log in to ChatCRM.
5. They enter name/email.
6. They sign paperwork.
7. Admin confirms them in ChatCRM Team tab.
8. Promote them to Acquisition Caller role.
9. Start live calling.
